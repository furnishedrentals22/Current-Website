    - **Leads Page:** Manual entry of leads with a multi-stage progress tracker. Leads are color-coded by strength. A Convert to Tenant feature should auto-fill the tenant creation form.
    - **Calendar Page:** A full calendar showing occupied/vacant days for all units, grouped by property.
